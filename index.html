About Us

Singing Mamas is dedicated to supporting mothers in Glamorgan, Bridgend, Port Talbot, and surrounding areas. Through the power of group singing, we aim to improve mental health, foster well-being, and create a strong community where mothers can connect and socialize.

Our sessions are designed to be inclusive, requiring no prior musical experience. By singing together, we not only enhance our own well-being but also create a positive environment for our children.

Improved Mental Health
