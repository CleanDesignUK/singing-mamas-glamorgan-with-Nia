Frequently Asked Questions

What is Singing Mamas?

Singing Mamas is a community-focused organization that uses group singing as a tool to enhance the mental and emotional well-being of mothers and their children.

Do I need any musical experience to join?

No prior musical experience is required. Our sessions are designed to be inclusive and welcoming to everyone.

How can I join a group?

You can join a group by signing up through our contact form below or by attending one of our taster sessions.

Are the sessions free?

Some sessions are free, while others may have a nominal fee to cover venue and material costs. Please contact us for more details.

What are the benefits of joining Singing Mamas?

Joining Singing Mamas offers numerous benefits including improved mental health, reduced stress, social connections, and a positive environment for your children.

Can I bring my child to the sessions?

Yes, our parent-child sessions are specifically designed to include your children, fostering a nurturing and engaging environment for both of you.
