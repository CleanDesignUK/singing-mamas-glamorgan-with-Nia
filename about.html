About Singing Mamas

Singing Mamas is a UK-based non-profit organization dedicated to enhancing women's well-being through group singing. Founded by Nia Prosser, we offer various programs and services to support women, particularly mothers and pregnant women, in Glamorgan, Bridgend, Port Talbot, and surrounding areas.

Our Leader - Nia Prosser

Hello everyone. I'm Nia Prosser, a mother of four beautiful children, two energetic dogs, and a loving wife. Living in Margam, South Wales, I have always embraced the rich Welsh tradition of singing. From my early days in school to my professional career, singing has been an integral part of my life.

With a background as a primary school teacher for 14 years, a childminder, a forest school leader, and currently a Monkey Music teacher, I have dedicated my life to education and fostering creativity in children. My passion for music therapy led me to lead Singing Mamas, aiming to share the profound benefits of singing with other mothers.

Despite battling depression, singing has been my anchor, keeping me resilient and positive. Research consistently shows that singing enhances physical, mental, and emotional well-being, making it an ideal activity for mothers seeking support and connection.

At Singing Mamas, we believe that every voice is valid and welcome. Our mission is to create a supportive community where mothers can socialize, improve their mental health, and enjoy the shared joy of singing with their children.

Our Mission & Vision

Singing Mamas is committed to enhancing women's mental health and well-being through the power of group singing. We provide a safe, non-judgmental space for mothers to connect, share experiences, and support each other. By fostering a sense of community and promoting social interaction, we aim to improve the overall quality of life for mothers and their children.

Our mission is to empower women through music, offering an accessible and enjoyable way to boost mental health, reduce stress, and build lasting friendships. Our vision is to expand our reach, creating more Singing Mamas groups across the UK and internationally, ensuring that every woman has the opportunity to experience the benefits of group singing.

Our Programs

Group Singing Sessions

Join our regular group singing sessions designed to enhance your mental well-being and foster a sense of community among mothers. Suitable for all skill levels.

Music Therapy Workshops

Our workshops focus on using music as a therapeutic tool to improve emotional and mental health. Led by certified music therapists.

Parent-Child Sessions

Engage in singing activities with your children, fostering stronger bonds and providing a positive environment for their emotional development.

Our Impact

Since our inception, Singing Mamas has positively impacted the lives of numerous women and their families. Participants have reported significant improvements in mood, reduced stress levels, and a strengthened sense of community.

By integrating singing into daily routines, mothers have found a therapeutic outlet that not only enhances their own well-being but also creates a nurturing environment for their children. Our partnerships with health services and collaborations with the NHS have enabled us to reach women most in need, providing free access to our services and incorporating singing as a form of support in healthcare settings.

Our training programs have empowered many women to become leaders in their communities, further expanding the reach and impact of Singing Mamas.
